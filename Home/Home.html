About Moon HR

Moon HR is one of the reputed manpower agencies in Nepal. We take a hands-on approach to meet the requirements of our clients. At Moon HR, we aim to know our clients, their business and what sort of manpower they require. As per the needs and requirements of the employing companies, we have been providing skilled, semi-skilled and unskilled labors.

We select the best candidates via formal interviews as interviews play a crucial role in hiring human resources and to check their verbal capabilities. After selecting the candidates, we train them to the highest levels and then employ them nationally as well as internationally. Moon HR is the best place to reach if you are seeking for talented candidates. Nonetheless, we are always there to take care of your HR management and invest our time in prolific and core HR operations.
